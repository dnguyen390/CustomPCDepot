$320 Light Gaming PC

Project Info

  • Client: Anida
  • Project Date: 12 March, 2020

Gaming Computer i5 3.1GHz, 12GB RAM, GTX 760, 1TB Hard Drive, Windows 10, Office 19

This was a $320 entry level gaming computer build that I put together for a client. The client's overall objective was to have a budget computer that can play older, low demand games on medium/high setting at 1080p with at least 60 FPS. Additionally, the client wanted the computer to handle some very light photo and video editing/rendering in Adobe Photoshop, Lightroom, Premier Pro, and After Effects. As a system builder, I acomplished her goal by focusing on delivering as much value for money, and price/performance possible. Below is a parts breakdown list of all the componets used in this project.


Parts List


  • CPU: Intel Core i5 2300 3.1 GHz Quad Core Processor
  • Motherboard: Intel H61 Motherboard
  • RAM: 12GB DDR3 1600 MHz
  • Video Card: EVGA GTX 760 2GB Gaming Graphics Card
  • Storage: 1TB Seagate 7200 RPM HDD
  • Power Supply: Seasonic 430W PSU
  • Case: Tempered Glass ATX Mid Tower
  • Operating System: Windows 10 Home
  • Additional Software: Microsoft Office 2019
